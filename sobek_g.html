Sobek

Appearance:

  • Man with the head of a crocodile
  • a headdress of feathers
  • a sun-disk

    • Sobek is the Crocodile God or Lord of Faiyum Oasis from Faiyum. Sobek was known as Suchos in Greek. The name Sobek also called in various spelling such as Sebek, Sochet, Sobk, Sobki or Soknopais. His appearance portrayed as a crocodile headed man with a headdress of feathers and a sun disk. He holds an ankh, representing his ability to undo evil and so cure ills. Sometimes, he depicted as a mummified crocodile or a crocodile itself.
